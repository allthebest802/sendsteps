💡 ADHD brain tips

Just keep dumping. Don't sort while you type. Get everything out first — even if it feels random or silly.
✅
Do Now = today only. If it doesn't absolutely need doing today, it goes in Do Later. Be strict.
🗑️
Let It Go is powerful. Worries, things other people need to sort, things you can't control — these belong here.
🔁
Do this regularly. Many ADHD brains feel better doing a brain dump first thing in the morning or before bed.
